Our Mission

Pawtner is a dedicated nonprofit that connects pets and people, giving animals a second chance and helping families grow with love.

What We Do

We provide a safe, ethical, and reliable alternative to general listing sites like Facebook, Preloved, Pets4Homes, and Gumtree.

Our platform connects responsible adopters with individuals who need to rehome their pets, making it easier for families to find the right companion while increasing the chances of pets finding their forever homes.

We offer a supportive and non-judgmental service, giving pet owners full control over the rehoming process. At the same time, we help reduce the number of animals entering shelters, freeing up valuable space and resources for pets that are abandoned, in urgent need, or require specialized care.

Building happy families through pet adoption

Adopting a pet brings joy, warmth, and lasting companionship. It creates a bond that transforms a home with love and brighter days ahead. Pawtner's mission is to connect pets in need with their forever families and share that unique kind of love with as many homes as possible.
